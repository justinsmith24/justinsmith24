    • Husson

    After high school I decided to attend Husson University in the five year Accounting/MBA program. It would allow me to receive a bachelors degree in accounting and a masters degree in business administration.

    Sports

    A big factor in my decision to attend Husson was the ability to play college basketball. Playing college basketabll was always a dream of mine and I was able to fufill it. My freshman season I started 24 of the 28 games and averaged 4 points, 3 rebounds, and 1 assist per game. My freshman year also ended with winning the North Atlantic Conference Championship and a National Tournament appearance. We ended up losing to Babson College who went on to win the Tournament. I went on to play 13 games the next season before I decided to leave.
